Location Recorder

How to use:

  • For single location: Click the "Get Location" button to retrieve your current location
  • For route: Enter start and end locations, then click "Get Route"
  • Your browser will ask for permission to access your location if needed
  • Once retrieved, your location/route will be displayed and saved to JSON
  • Click "Download JSON" to save the data to your computer
